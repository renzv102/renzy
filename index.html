Welcome To Veerachai Court, Chiang Mai
Having operated since 1988, Veerachai Court offers an ideal accommodation in the form of an apartment in Chiang Mai with low room rates, which vary according to the season and the duration of your stay. Whether it is a one-day, short- or long-term stay, a reasonable room rate will be tailor-made just for you.


Located in the central downtown of Chiang Mai with an easy access to all major attractions, including business, nightlife, entertainment and shopping centres, yet in a peaceful atmosphere, Veerachai Court is a perfect base for business people and tourists alike.
